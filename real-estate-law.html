Helping You With Your Real Estate Legal Needs

TKuchar Law is the best go-to law firm for Israel-based or overseas English-speaking clientele looking to sell, purchase, or lease residential properties in the State of Israel. Our law office conducts the necessary due diligence prior to the signing of any real estate contract to ensure that our clientsâ€™ interests are fully guarded.

Our law office works very closely with other stakeholders in the real property industry such as real estate brokers, mortgage brokers, appraisers, and other real estate and property development professionals with our client at the center of our relationship with these providers. With TKuchar Law, you can sleep well at night knowing that your real estate legal matters are handled professionally and with your best interests on top.

Our services include, but are not limited to, the following:

- Preparation of Powers of Attorney and other related legal instruments necessary to legally complete real estate transactions;
- Client-directed contract negotiation in relation to the purchase, sale, or lease of real estate property;
- Conduct of due diligence activities prior to the purchase, sale, or lease of real estate property;
- Review and signing of contracts to purchase, sell, or lease of real property on behalf of clients, filing the appropriate tax report for the transaction, and facilitating payments related to the transaction; and
- Facilitate transfer of title from buyer to seller upon mutual fulfillment of contract obligations.
