Meet Tanya

A Firm You Can Trust

Dealing with legal matters related to real estate, wills and estates, and business requires a top-notch lawyer with mastery in those areas of law. Tanya Kuchar is one of the country's leading lawyers in real estate, wills & estates, and business law and can help you with any matter related to those areas with her depth of experience, width of knowledge, and proven efficiency.


Tanya Kuchar is a leading legal practitioner in the field of wills & estates and property law. She has been a member of the bar since 2002, and has had exposure to this area of law since then. Her practice specializes in helping the Anglo-speaking community in Israel.


As one of the country's top practitioners in the field of wills, estates, and succession law, Tanya can help you prepare, in a legal sense, an Enduring Power of Attorney (POA), also called a 'living will'. This document will express your personal, financial, and medical affairs in case you become unable to manage these affairs yourself. She will walk you through the intricacies of the process and ensure that your living will is truly reflective of your personal preferences for the guidance of those who you intend to empower with this legal authority.
