Meet Tanya

About Tanya Kuchar

Tanya Kuchar is a successful lawyer with a focused practice in real estate law, and wills & estates. She obtained her law degree from the University of Bar Ilan, and became a member of the Israeli Bar in 2002.

She has worked for numerous real estate and trade firms as legal counsel, with a notable stint in one of New York's most prestigious precious gems trading firms. She has also worked for many renowned Israeli law firms prior to setting up her own firm in 2014.

Tanya started her own boutique law firm in 2014, with a specialized practice in the fields of real estate law, and wills & estates law. She has helped many clients from the Anglo-speaking community on matters of real estate law.

Her wills & estates law practice is focused on living wills, where she helps clients document their personal, medical, and financial decisions in the event of incapacity.

Her practice also focuses on Israel's growing Anglo-speaking community who needs a lawyer who understands the nuances of their new life in Israel. Tanya, who is a native of South Africa, thoroughly understands the unique needs of this community.
